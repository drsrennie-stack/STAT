The Tool Hub

When your grade can't wait.

The companion tool hub for the STAT Success Workbook. Every interactive tool, organized to mirror the workbook flow. Open one, do the work, the page does the math for you.

If you bought the workbook

Every workbook section that has a matching online tool is flagged in the book. You can complete each worksheet with pencil and paper if you prefer, and the workbook is designed to stand alone. But these tools do the scoring and interpretation automatically, which most readers will find easier and more honest than scoring themselves. We recommend you use them.
