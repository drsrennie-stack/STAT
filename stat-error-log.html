How to use it

Log one row for every wrong answer: what you thought, what is correct, why you missed it, and what you will remember. Add as many rows as you need. A week later, try the same question again without looking. When you get it right, you can delete the row. When you are done, print the page (or save it as a PDF) and keep it with your workbook. Your rows are saved in this browser until you clear them.
