STAT Success Cohort · Week 7 Tool

Final Exam Triage.

You will not study everything. The work is choosing what to study, with eyes open. This tool calculates a priority order from your topic weights and your honest confidence, and produces a study allocation for the time you actually have. Takes 20 to 30 minutes. Auto-saves as you go.

How to use this tool

Section 1, enter the days until your final and the hours per day you can realistically study. Section 2, list each topic on your final, its weight on the exam, and your honest confidence. Section 3, click Calculate to see your priority order and study hours per topic. Section 4, review your day-by-day plan. Print or download the result.
