Your team charter.

Build this together during your Week 1 live session. Your team agrees on how you will work together for the next 8 weeks, and everyone signs. Your facilitator will fill this in on screen share while the team agrees on each answer.

How to use this tool

Your facilitator will lead this during the Week 1 live session. One charter is built per team. At the end, use Print or Download to save a copy for your records. Your facilitator will also send the completed charter to everyone. Your work auto-saves as you go.

05

Attendance commitments

Agreements the team makes about showing up. Check each box the team commits to. These are shared commitments, not individual ones.

  • We will attend all 8 live sessions for the full 90 minutes, on time, with very rare exceptions.
  • Camera on whenever possible. We recognize that being visually present changes the quality of team work. We agree to turn on camera unless we have a specific reason not to.
  • Notify the team if we will be late or absent. Communication with the team is part of showing up, even when we cannot physically attend.
  • Watch the recording within 48 hours if we miss. We will not show up the next week unprepared.
06

Confidentiality

What gets shared in your team stays in your team. These agreements protect the honesty that makes recovery work possible.

  • What happens in team stays in team. We do not share identifying details about teammates' academic or personal situations with anyone outside the team.
  • No screenshots or recordings of teammates. Only the facilitator's session recording, which is shared with the team for makeup purposes only.
  • Safety exception. If a facilitator is concerned about a team member's safety, they may break confidentiality to connect that student with appropriate help. This protects us, not the reverse.
  • No sharing of each other's grades or academic records outside the team. Specific grade data stays private between the individual, the team, and the facilitator.
