Technology and logistics.

The practical stuff. How to join sessions, where the tools live, what to do when something breaks, and how to reach your facilitator.

How to access your live sessions

Your live sessions happen on Zoom. You'll find the Zoom link in each weekly session lesson (for example, Lesson 2.4 is Live Session 1). Click the link at the scheduled session time to join.

We recommend joining 2 to 5 minutes before the session starts to handle any audio or video setup. If this is your first time using Zoom, open the link 15 minutes early to give yourself time to install the Zoom app if needed.

Sessions are 90 minutes. Show up on camera if possible. Being present visually changes the quality of team conversation in ways audio-only can't replicate.

How to find each week's tools

Each week's section in this course contains the tools you need for that week. The pattern is consistent:

  • One "This week's STAT" overview lesson
  • Pre-read or case materials (starting Week 2)
  • The Current state audit tool (weekly)
  • The Live Session access link
  • The decision plan tool (weekly)
  • Any optional deeper tools specific to that week

You can work through each section sequentially. Tools auto-save your progress in your browser, so you can stop mid-tool and return later to finish.

If something breaks

If a tool won't load, won't save, or behaves unexpectedly, try these steps in order:

  1. Refresh the page.
  2. Clear your browser cache and reload.
  3. Try a different browser. Chrome, Firefox, and Safari all work; Internet Explorer does not.
  4. Try a different device (computer vs. phone vs. tablet).

If none of those fix it, email your facilitator with a description of what you were doing when it broke and what browser you were using. We'll sort it out.

Saving your work

Every tool saves your progress to your browser automatically. You don't need to click a save button. If you close the tab and come back later, your work should still be there.

i

Important

If you clear your browser cache or switch devices, your saved data may not carry over. For important tools (like your commitment contract and your weekly audits), use the Download button to save a backup copy to your computer.
